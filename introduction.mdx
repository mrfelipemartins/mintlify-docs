## Quickstart

<Note>
Zapply is a non-official WhatsApp API. It is not affiliated with WhatsApp or Meta in any way.
</Note>

<Warning>
Zapply devices are not intented for bulk messaging. If you need to send bulk messages, please contact us for a dedicated solution.
</Warning>
